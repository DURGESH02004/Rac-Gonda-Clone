Department of Civil Engineering

The Department of Civil Engineering is dedicated to providing high-quality technical education, encouraging innovation, and preparing industry-ready civil engineers. It offers a 4-year B.Tech. program with an intake of 60 students. The department features modern classrooms, state-of-the-art laboratories, and access to advanced software tools for structural analysis and design. With a curriculum that blends theoretical foundations and practical applications, students gain knowledge in construction, environmental engineering, transportation, and geotechnics. Emphasis is placed on sustainable development, research, and hands-on learning. The department supports academic excellence and skill development through workshops, internships, and industry collaborations. Since its establishment, Civil Engineering has played a crucial role in nation-building, producing responsible engineers who contribute to infrastructure, safety, and environmental sustainability in a rapidly developing world.
